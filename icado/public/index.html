About

who we are
We are a community based and non-profit organization established to bring hope for vulnerable communities in the sub region increase their access and use of affordable micro finance, agriculture extension services, use of good agronomy and support, market information, capacity building and community empowerment skills with the aims of having sustainable communities and livelihood development improved.
Mission
To promote development innovation in the community by increasing access and use of affordable financial services, market information, extension agricultural services, and capacity skills development programs.
Vision
A sustainable community embracing patterns of change in terms of livelihood development
Goals
Fueling social-economic transformation in the community through livelihood development programs

    WOWEN EMPOWERMENT PROJECT
    (ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND SMALL –SCALE BUSINESS STARTUP SKILLS)

    PROJECT SUMMARY

    The Rural Women Empowerment Project focuses on training, microfinance and mentorship of vulnerable women and their families in rural communities of Iganga District with basic entrepreneurship and small scale business startups skills and maintenance of the already existing small business entities Uganda is a developing country with a high number of vulnerable women like the, widows, sing mother, women affected and infected with HIV/AIDS, grandmothers, domestic violence victims and women living in extreme poverty with high level of vulnerability most women are illerate, have no clear sources of income, can’t afford meals for their children and those lack opportunities to thrive, However, they have the potential to change and get themselves out of the situation if supported Women vulnerability arises from their unequal social status and from unequal power relations which accord women less access to control over resources and other assets than men and these finds them in informal sector with lack of basic entrepreneurship skills, sufficient access to capital markets, collateral and poor saving culture all of which are necessary for helping them start and grow their small business. There is need to support and aid vulnerable women with entrepreneurship skills to reduce their economic poverty, By contributing to Rural Women Empowerment Program in Uganda, you will be able to contribute to alleviating poverty out of the many vulnerable women and their families to enable them create sustainable livelihood.

    PROJECT DESCRIPTION

    The Rural Women Empowerment Program is providing women groups with basic business entrepreneurship training and mentorship on managing small business startups, advancing small affordable loans schemes for developing and startups of small scale business development, instilling saving culture among the groups thriving at having sustainable incomes and reduce the level of vulnerability among the women. Under this approach, it involves grouping vulnerable women in groups of 10-15 members, meeting and interacting with them in their communities and creates interactive training method that will create chance for everyone to participate in the training and that will improve women’s entrepreneurship and small scale business startups. The project will also disseminate basic health promotion information and agriculture extension services and environmental conservation to the women alongside supporting them to start up small business, manage their small startup loan and extend mentorship. The core objective is to empower vulnerable women to fight poverty and become self-sufficient and improve their livelihood.

    COMMUNITY IMPACT

    The program will reduce the women’s financial vulnerability and a rise their ability to afford basic necessities of life. The small business will strengthened by organization, development patterns, volunteers, interns and acquired basic entrepreneurship literacy. TO THE DEVELOPMENT PATTERNS, VOLUNTEERS, INTERNS AND WELL-WISHERS Our patterns in development, volunteers and interns and well-wishers will learn about sustainable development model of empowering rural women and dynamics of micro-financing /micro-enterprise for women at grass root level. They will also learn on how vulnerable women in Uganda make a living and care for their families while contributing to rural women empowerment programs Share experiences with the rural communities and families COMMUNITY EMPOERMENT SKILLS PROJECT (TAILORING AND FASHION DESIGN AND HAND CRAFT MAKING)

    PROJECT SUMMARY

    The community empowerment skills project will focus on training and mentorship for vulnerable communities focusing on single mothers, school dropouts, PWDs, youth and women group’s disadvantaged men and people affected and infected with HIV/AIDs in rural communities of Iganga District and Busoga sub region with basic vocational skills for small scale business startups and development and new innovation in the sector. Uganda’s population is characterized with a high number of informal sectors who are vulnerable and without basic vocational skills to thrive them and sustain themselves Most of them are illerate, have no skills and have no clear incomes for their families, they lack appropriate opportunities to thrive, however, they have the potential to change and get themselves out of the situation if only given support of acquiring necessary basic skills which can necessitate them for self-sustainability and improve on livelihood and small scale business startups. Vulnerability arises from the unequal social status from background, stigma, educational background, inadequate entrepreneurship skills, and sufficient access to capital markets to facilitating these vulnerable groups to acquire skills and grow themselves There is need to support and aid these vulnerable groups in the rural communities reduce on their economic poverty. By contributing to the Community Empowerment Skills Program in Uganda you will be able to contribute to an alleviating poverty out of the many vulnerable groups and their families and enable them to create their own sustainable livelihood.

    PROJECT DESCRIPTION

    The Community Empowerment Skills Program will be equipping basic vocational skills training to vulnerable groups of people in the rural community focusing on tailoring and fashion design and hand crafting, managing small scale business enterprises startups and capacity and mentorship building aiming at having sustainable incomes and reduce the vulnerability in the communities Under this program, the approach of delivery of trainings and services to our target beneficiaries (vulnerable groups) will involve identifying the different groups of vulnerable people in the communities, group them into 10-15 members and train them at the organization Community Empowerment Center (CEC).The training period for every group shall be 3 Months then after which a certificate of completion shall be awarded. Proper arrangements and support shall be made to see that all these groups startup small scale business entities. The kind of training will be interactive in nature, use of demonstrations and illustrations which will provide Hands on Skills for our vulnerable groups. The training will also disseminate basic health information, environmental conservation and agriculture extension services and how to start up. The core aim and objectives of the project is to empower different vulnerable groups of people in the community with basic Hands on Skills for self-sustainability, improved livelihood incomes and reduction on the level of vulnerability

    COMMUNITY IMPACT

    The program will reduce on the level of skills vulnerability to the different groups as it will equip basic vocational skills for startups. Self- sustainability and also afford basic necessities of their life and families. The small set projects will be supported and strengthened by the organization, development patterns, volunteers, interns and acquired basic knowledge from the project TO THE DEVELOPMENT PATTERNS, VOLUNTEERS, INTERNS AND WELL-WISHERS The development patterns, volunteers, interns and well-wishers will be able to learn on how to create sustainable development model of empowering rural communities through basic training in skilling and dynamics of managing startups of small scale entities at grass root level They will also learn on how vulnerable groups make a living and care for their families while contributing to the Community Empowerment Skills Program. Share experiences with the different vulnerable groups and communities.
